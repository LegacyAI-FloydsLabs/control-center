# Legacy AI Team Builder System

Build high-performing SaaS teams from repository analysis with expert-level prompts designed for 100% success.

## Overview

The Legacy AI Team Builder is an advanced AI system that analyzes repository reports and constructs optimal SaaS teams with:
- **Development Team**: Matched to project completion and tech stack
- **Ship Readiness Team**: Focused on QA, DevOps, and launch preparation
- **Marketing & Sales Team**: Aligned with target market and business model

Each team member is configured as a top-level expert with the knowledge and tools to succeed with unwavering confidence.

## Quick Start

1. **Run the team builder:**
   ```bash
   python build_teams_from_report.py
   ```

2. **Paste your repository report** when prompted (use the template in `repository_report_template.md`)

3. **Receive three team files** with expert-level prompts for each role

## Files Generated

For each project, the system creates:

- `[project-name]-development-team.md` - Development team configuration
- `[project-name]-ship-readiness-team.md` - QA and launch team
- `[project-name]-marketing-sales-team.md` - Growth and revenue team

Each file contains:
- Complete team roster with Legacy AI naming conventions
- Expert-level prompts following the 8-step framework
- Clear responsibilities and success metrics
- Collaboration protocols

## Expert Prompt Framework

Every team member receives a prompt with this structure:

1. Deeply understand the project's true goal
2. Break problems into fundamental principles
3. Think step-by-step with perfect logic
4. Consider multiple approaches and choose optimal
5. Anticipate weaknesses and counterarguments
6. Generate the absolute best result
7. Ruthlessly self-critique
8. Fix every flaw before delivery

## Team Composition Logic

### Development Team
- **0-30% completion**: 4 members (early stage)
- **31-60% completion**: 6 members (active development)
- **61-85% completion**: 8 members (completion phase)
- **86-100% completion**: 10 members (polishing phase)

### Ship Readiness Team
- **0-50% completion**: 3 members (early prep)
- **51-80% completion**: 5 members (advanced testing)
- **81-100% completion**: 7 members (full launch)

### Marketing & Sales Team
- **B2B**: 7 members (enterprise focus)
- **B2C**: 6 members (consumer focus)
- **B2B2C**: 8 members (hybrid approach)
